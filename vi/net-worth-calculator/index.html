Tinh Tong Tai San Rong Cua Ban Mien Phi

Tai san rong la con so quan trong nhat trong tai chinh ca nhan. No cho ban biet chinh xac vi tri cua minh bang cach cong tat ca nhung gi ban so huu va tru di nhung gi ban no. Theo doi theo thoi gian cho thay ban dang xay dung tai san hay tut lai.

Tinh Tai San Rong — Mien Phi

Cach Tinh Tai San Rong

Cong thuc rat don gian — cong tat ca tai san, tru tat ca no phai tra, va ket qua la tai san rong cua ban:

Tai San Rong = Tong Tai San - Tong No Phai Tra

Tai san thanh khoan la nhung thu ban co the chuyen doi thanh tien mat nhanh chong: tai khoan tiet kiem, co phieu, trai phieu, tien dien tu va tai khoan huu tri. Nhung tai san nay quyet dinh lo trinh doc lap tai chinh cua ban.

Tai san kho thanh khoan co gia tri nhung kho ban: bat dong san, xe co, von doanh nghiep va do suu tam. Chung dong gop vao tong tai san rong nhung khong de dang trang trai chi phi sinh hoat.

NetWorthCast theo doi ca hai rieng biet va cho phep ban gan ty le tang truong rieng cho tung tai san — vi vay danh muc co phieu co the tang 8% trong khi nha cua ban tang gia 3%.

Nhung Gi Duoc Tinh La Tai San

Bao gom moi thu co gia tri ma ban so huu:

  • Tien mat & tiet kiem — tai khoan thanh toan, tai khoan tiet kiem, quy du phong
  • Dau tu — co phieu, trai phieu, quy chi so, ETF, quy tuong ho
  • Tai khoan huu tri — 401(k), IRA, Roth IRA, luong huu
  • Tien dien tu — Bitcoin, Ethereum va cac tai san ky thuat so khac
  • Bat dong san — nha o chinh, bat dong san cho thue, dat
  • Phuong tien — o to, xe may, tau
  • Khac — von doanh nghiep, do suu tam, trang suc, tai san tri tue

Sau do tru no phai tra: so du the chap, no sinh vien, vay mua xe, no the tin dung, vay ca nhan, no y te va bat ky khoan tien nao khac ban no.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Tai san rong la gi?

Tai san rong la tong gia tri cua moi thu ban so huu (tai san) tru di moi thu ban no (no phai tra). Day la thuoc do tot nhat ve suc khoe tai chinh tong the. Tai san rong duong co nghia la tai san vuot qua no, trong khi tai san rong am co nghia la ban no nhieu hon nhung gi ban so huu.

Lam the nao de tinh tai san rong?

Tai San Rong = Tong Tai San - Tong No Phai Tra. Cong tat ca tai san (tien mat, tiet kiem, dau tu, bat dong san, xe co) va tru tat ca no phai tra (the chap, no sinh vien, no the tin dung, vay mua xe). Ket qua la tai san rong cua ban.

Nhung gi duoc tinh la tai san va no phai tra?

Tai san bao gom tien mat, tai khoan tiet kiem, co phieu, trai phieu, 401(k), IRA, tien dien tu, bat dong san, xe co va tai san ca nhan. No phai tra bao gom the chap, no sinh vien, vay mua xe, so du the tin dung, vay ca nhan va bat ky khoan no nao khac.

Tai san rong trung binh theo tuoi la bao nhieu?

Muc tai san rong trung binh bien dong nhieu, nhung muc tieu chung la: ~$20.000 o tuoi 25, ~$100.000 o tuoi 30, ~$250.000 o tuoi 35, ~$500.000 o tuoi 40, ~$1.000.000 o tuoi 50 va ~$2.000.000 o tuoi 60. Nhung con so nay phu thuoc nhieu vao thu nhap, ty le tiet kiem va loi nhuan dau tu.
