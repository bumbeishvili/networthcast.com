Coast FIRE

Khi Nao Ban Co The Ngung Tiet Kiem Cho Huu Tri?

Coast FIRE la thoi diem khoan dau tu cua ban da du lon de chi bang lai kep cung se dua ban den doc lap tai chinh — ngay ca khi ban khong tiet kiem them mot dong nao. Ban van lam viec de trang trai chi phi hien tai, nhung huu tri da duoc dam bao.

Tinh So Coast FIRE Cua Ban — Mien Phi

Coast FIRE La Gi?

Coast FIRE la mot cot moc tren con duong den doc lap tai chinh. Do la thoi diem ma cac khoan dau tu hien tai cua ban, nho suc manh cua lai kep, se dat den so FIRE day du vao thoi diem ban den tuoi nghi huu truyen thong — ma khong can them mot dong nao nua.

Khi ban dat Coast FIRE, ap luc tiet kiem manh me se bien mat. Ban van can kiem du de trang trai chi phi sinh hoat hien tai, nhung moi dong ban kiem duoc co the dung de song cuoc song hien tai thay vi tai tro cho tuong lai xa xoi.

Hay nghi the nay: FIRE thong thuong co nghia la ban co the ngung lam viec hoan toan. Coast FIRE co nghia la ban co the ngung tiet kiem hoan toan. Ban van lam viec, nhung ban tu do chon cong viec luong thap hon ma ban yeu thich, lam ban thoi gian, lam tu do, hoac theo duoi cac du an dam me — vi lai kep dang lam phan nang phia sau.

Cong Thuc Coast FIRE

So Coast FIRE cho ban biet ban can dau tu bao nhieu hom nay de chi bang lai kep cung dat den muc tieu FIRE vao tuoi nghi huu:

So Coast FIRE = So FIRE ÷ (1 + r)n

Trong do r la loi nhuan thuc hang nam ky vong (sau lam phat) va n la so nam cho den tuoi nghi huu muc tieu.

Vi Du Voi So Thuc

Gia su ban chi $5.000/thang ($60.000/nam). So FIRE cua ban la $60.000 × 25 = $1.500.000. Ban 30 tuoi va nham nghi huu o tuoi 60, nen n = 30 nam. Gia dinh loi nhuan thuc 7%:

$1.500.000 ÷ (1,07)30 = $197.000

Neu ban co $197.000 dau tu o tuoi 30, ban da dat Coast FIRE. Lai kep o muc 7% se bien so do thanh $1,5 trieu vao tuoi 60 — khong can tiet kiem them.

Tai Sao Coast FIRE Quan Trong

Dat Coast FIRE thay doi cuoc choi, ngay ca khi ban con nhieu nam nua moi dat FIRE day du:

  • Giam ap luc tai chinh — ban khong con can toi da hoa ty le tiet kiem hoac chay theo thu nhap cao hon chi de nghi huu
  • Mo ra su linh hoat nghe nghiep — chuyen sang vai tro luong thap hon ma ban thuc su thich, lam ban thoi gian, hoac khoi nghiep ma khong rui ro huu tri
  • Tao dieu kien cho cac du an dam me — chap nhan giam luong de lam cong viec y nghia, tinh nguyen, du lich, hoac quay lai truong hoc
  • Giam cang thang — biet rang huu tri da duoc dam bao bang toan hoc mang lai su an tam to lon
  • Cung cap muc tieu som co the dat duoc — FIRE day du co the cam thay xa voi; Coast FIRE la cot moc co the dat duoc chung minh toan hoc dang hoat dong

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Coast FIRE la gi?

Coast FIRE la thoi diem ma cac khoan dau tu hien tai cua ban se tang len den so FIRE day du vao tuoi nghi huu chi bang lai kep — ngay ca khi ban khong tiet kiem them mot dong nao. Ban van lam viec de trang trai chi phi sinh hoat hien tai, nhung viec tai tro huu tri da hoan tat.

Lam the nao de tinh so Coast FIRE?

So Coast FIRE = So FIRE ÷ (1 + loi nhuan ky vong hang nam)^so nam den khi nghi huu. Vi du, voi muc tieu FIRE $1,5 trieu, loi nhuan thuc 7%, va con 25 nam nua, so Coast FIRE cua ban la $1.500.000 ÷ (1,07)^25 = khoang $276.000.

Su khac biet giua Coast FIRE va FIRE thong thuong la gi?

FIRE thong thuong co nghia la dau tu cua ban trang trai moi chi phi va ban khong bao gio can lam viec nua. Coast FIRE co nghia la lai kep cuoi cung se dua ban den do, nhung ban van can kiem thu nhap cho chi phi hien tai. Coast FIRE dat duoc som hon nhieu va la cot moc tren con duong den FIRE day du.

Toi co the nghi huu sau khi dat Coast FIRE khong?

Khong hoan toan. Coast FIRE co nghia la ban co the ngung tiet kiem cho huu tri, nhung ban van can thu nhap cho chi phi hang ngay. Uu diem: ban can it thu nhap hon nhieu, nen ban co the chuyen sang lam ban thoi gian, lam tu do, hoac cong viec luong thap hon ma ban yeu thich.

Nen dung ty le loi nhuan bao nhieu cho phep tinh Coast FIRE?

Hau het cac phep tinh su dung loi nhuan thuc 7% (da dieu chinh lam phat), dua tren muc trung binh dai han cua danh muc co phieu da dang hoa. Nhung nguoi lap ke hoach than trong hon su dung 5-6%. NetWorthCast cho phep ban dat ty le loi nhuan rieng cho tung tai san de du bao chinh xac hon.
