Chenh Lech Dia Ly La Gi?

Chenh lech dia ly — con goi la geo-arbitrage — co nghia la tan dung su chenh lech chi phi sinh hoat giua noi ban kiem tien va noi ban chi tieu. Neu ban kiem luong My lam viec tu xa va song o thanh pho noi tien thue, thuc pham va y te chi bang mot phan nho so voi My, ty le tiet kiem cua ban se tang vot.

Khai niem nay cuc ky pho bien trong cong dong FIRE (Doc Lap Tai Chinh, Nghi Huu Som) vi no truc tiep tan cong bien so lon nhat trong phuong trinh FIRE: chi phi. Chi phi thap hon co nghia la so FIRE thap hon, ty le tiet kiem cao hon va it nam hon de ban tu do tai chinh.

Ban khong can phai roi khoi dat nuoc. Chuyen tu San Francisco sang Boise, hoac tu New York sang Raleigh, la chenh lech dia ly trong nuoc. Nhung loi ich lon nhat den tu viec chuyen quoc te — kiem bang USD trong khi song o Dong Nam A, Dong Au hoac My La-tinh.

Xem Xet Thue Cho Chenh Lech Dia Ly

Thue co the thanh cong hay that bai mot chien luoc chenh lech dia ly. Day la nhung dieu can biet:

  • Thue lanh tho: Mot so quoc gia (Georgia, Thai Lan, Panama) chi danh thue thu nhap kiem trong lanh tho cua ho. Thu nhap tu nuoc ngoai — nhu luong lam tu xa My hoac loi nhuan dau tu — duoc mien thue.
  • Visa du muc ky thuat so: Cac quoc gia nhu Croatia, Bo Dao Nha, Tay Ban Nha va Estonia cung cap visa dac biet cho nguoi lam tu xa, doi khi voi dieu khoan thue thuan loi.
  • Thue toan cau: My danh thue cong dan tren thu nhap toan cau bat ke ho song o dau. Tuy nhien, Mien Tru Thu Nhap Kiem Duoc O Nuoc Ngoai (FEIE) cho phep ban mien toi $126.500 thu nhap kiem duoc o nuoc ngoai (2024). Tin Dung Thue Nuoc Ngoai co the bu dap thue da nop o nuoc ngoai.
  • Hiep dinh thue: Nhieu quoc gia co hiep dinh thue song phuong de tranh danh thue hai lan. Kiem tra xem quoc gia goc cua ban co hiep dinh voi diem den muc tieu khong.
  • Che do dac biet: Chuong trinh Cu Tru Khong Thuong Xuyen (NHR) cua Bo Dao Nha cung cap thue suat co dinh 20% tren mot so loai thu nhap cho cu dan moi. Cac chuong trinh tuong tu ton tai o Italy, Hy Lap va Malta.

Luon tham van chuyen gia thue quoc te truoc khi di chuyen. Khoan tiet kiem tu chenh lech dia ly co the rat lon — nhung chi khi ban cau truc dung cach.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Chenh lech dia ly la gi?

Chenh lech dia ly (geo-arbitrage) la chien luoc kiem thu nhap bang dong tien manh — nhu USD, GBP hoac EUR — trong khi song o noi co chi phi sinh hoat thap hon nhieu. Dieu nay toi da hoa ty le tiet kiem va tang toc con duong den doc lap tai chinh.

Ban co the tiet kiem bao nhieu voi chenh lech dia ly?

Khoan tiet kiem bien dong nhieu, nhung chuyen tu thanh pho chi phi cao nhu New York hoac San Francisco sang thanh pho chi phi thap nhu Tbilisi hoac Chiang Mai co the giam chi phi hang thang 50-70%. Voi muc luong $80.000, dieu nay co the dong nghia voi tiet kiem them $30.000 moi nam va dat FIRE som hon 6-10 nam.

Toi co phai roi khoi dat nuoc de su dung chenh lech dia ly khong?

Khong. Chenh lech dia ly cung hoat dong trong nuoc. Chuyen tu San Francisco sang Boise, hoac tu New York sang Raleigh, co the giam dang ke chi phi sinh hoat trong khi van giu cung mui gio va he thong thue. Chuyen quoc te mang lai khoan tiet kiem lon hon nhung can nhieu ke hoach hon.

Thue khi song o nuoc ngoai thi sao?

Xu ly thue thay doi theo quoc gia. Mot so quoc gia nhu Georgia va Thai Lan khong danh thue thu nhap tu nuoc ngoai. Nhung quoc gia khac nhu Bo Dao Nha cung cap che do thue dac biet cho cu dan moi. Cong dan My bi danh thue tren thu nhap toan cau nhung co the su dung Mien Tru Thu Nhap Kiem Duoc O Nuoc Ngoai (FEIE) de mien toi $126.500 thu nhap kiem duoc o nuoc ngoai.

Thanh pho nao tot nhat cho chenh lech dia ly?

Khong co thanh pho tot nhat duy nhat — no phu thuoc vao uu tien cua ban. Tbilisi co chi phi thap nhat va thue suat 1%. Chiang Mai co co so ha tang tuyet voi cho du muc ky thuat so.
