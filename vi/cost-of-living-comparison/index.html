Cach Hoat Dong

Cong cu so sanh chi phi sinh hoat cua NetWorthCast duoc tich hop vao du bao tai chinh cua ban, de ban co the thay tac dong thuc te len tai san rong theo thoi gian.

  • Nhap thu nhap cua ban — hang thang hoac hang nam, bang bat ky dong tien nao. Cong cu chuyen doi moi thu sang USD de so sanh.
  • Chon quy mo gia dinh — mot nguoi, cap doi hoac gia dinh. Chi phi duoc dieu chinh dua tren quy mo ho gia dinh voi he so thuc te (cap doi khong ton gap doi mot nguoi).
  • Duyet hoac so sanh thanh pho — xem tat ca 44 thanh pho xep hang theo tong chi phi hang thang, hoac so sanh hai thanh pho canh nhau voi bang phan tich chi tiet theo hang muc.
  • Xem ban du kha nang o dau — cong cu danh dau cac thanh pho noi thu nhap cua ban thoai mai trang trai chi phi va hien thi so tien tiet kiem du kien hang thang o moi dia diem.
  • Ket noi voi ke hoach FIRE — xem viec chuyen noi o thay doi so FIRE va ngay nghi huu cua ban nhu the nao.

Cac So Sanh Pho Bien

New York City so voi Lisbon

Mot nguoi o NYC chi khoang $4.800/thang cho cac nhu cau thiet yeu. Cung muc song do o Lisbon chi khoang $2.200/thang — giam 54%. Rieng tien thue nha giam tu ~$2.800 xuong ~$1.100. Che do thue cu tru khong thuong xuyen (NHR) cua Bo Dao Nha co the giam them thue suat hieu qua cua ban.

San Francisco so voi Bangkok

San Francisco trung binh $5.500/thang cho mot nguoi. Bangkok chi khoang $1.400/thang — re hon khoang 75%. Chi phi thuc pham giam dang ke, va y te chat luong cao voi gia chi bang mot phan nho so voi My. Visa du muc ky thuat so cua Thai Lan giup o dai han kha thi.

London so voi Tbilisi

London ton khoang $4.200/thang. Tbilisi trung binh $1.100/thang — tiet kiem 74%. Georgia ap dung he thong thue lanh tho (thu nhap tu nuoc ngoai khong bi danh thue) va mot trong nhung che do visa than thien nhat cho nguoi lam viec tu xa.

Dubai so voi Bali

Tien thue cao o Dubai day chi phi hang thang len khoang $3.600 cho mot nguoi, mac du thue thu nhap 0%. Bali trung binh $1.300/thang voi loi song tuong tu. Danh doi: Dubai cung cap co so ha tang dang cap the gioi, trong khi Bali cung cap thien nhien va nhip song cham hon — ca hai deu co xu ly thue thuan loi cho nhieu quoc tich.

Cho Nguoi Lam Viec Tu Xa & Du Muc Ky Thuat So

Neu ban kiem bang USD, EUR hoac GBP va co the lam viec o bat cu dau, so sanh chi phi sinh hoat tro thanh cong cu tai chinh manh me nhat cua ban. Chien luoc nay — kiem bang dong tien manh trong khi chi tieu bang dong tien yeu hon — duoc goi la chenh lech dia ly.

Xem Xet Ve Visa

Nhieu thanh pho trong danh sach cua chung toi hien cung cap visa du muc ky thuat so hoac giay phep lam tu do: Bo Dao Nha, Tay Ban Nha, Georgia, Estonia, Thai Lan, Colombia va UAE deu co chuong trinh rieng. Loai visa anh huong den nghia vu thue cua ban, nen hay luon kiem tra yeu cau dia phuong.

Anh Huong Thue

Chuyen ra nuoc ngoai khong tu dong loai bo nghia vu thue tai quoc gia goc cua ban. Cong dan My bi danh thue tren thu nhap toan cau. Cu dan EU co the kich hoat cu tru thue sau 183 ngay. Cac quoc gia nhu Georgia, Dubai va Paraguay cung cap thue lanh tho hoac thue thu nhap bang khong — nhung cac quy tac rat tinh te. Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

So sanh chi phi sinh hoat la gi?

So sanh chi phi sinh hoat do luong muc do dat do de duy tri muc song tuong tu o cac thanh pho hoac quoc gia khac nhau. No bao gom thue nha, thuc pham, giao thong, dien nuoc, y te va thue. NetWorthCast so sanh 44 thanh pho tren 11 khu vuc de ban co the thay noi nao tien cua ban co gia tri nhat.

Cong cu tinh chi phi sinh hoat hoat dong nhu the nao?

Nhap thu nhap va quy mo gia dinh cua ban. Cong cu tinh toan dieu chinh chi phi cho moi thanh pho dua tren quy mo ho gia dinh va hien thi bang phan tich hang thang ve thue nha, thuc pham, giao thong, dien nuoc, y te, chi phi khac va thue uoc tinh. So sanh hai thanh pho canh nhau hoac duyet tat ca 44 thanh pho xep hang theo tong chi phi hang thang.

Thanh pho nao co chi phi sinh hoat thap nhat?

Trong 44 thanh pho cua chung toi, Chiang Mai, Sofia, Goa va Da Nang lien tuc xep hang la nhung noi gia ca phai chang nhat. Nhung thanh pho re nhat cho ban phu thuoc vao loi song, quy mo gia dinh va hang muc chi phi nao quan trong nhat voi ban.

Chi phi sinh hoat co bao gom thue khong?

Co. NetWorthCast bao gom thue suat hieu qua uoc tinh cho moi thanh pho de ban so sanh suc mua thuc nhan, khong chi la chi phi truoc thue. Thue suat dao dong tu 0% o Dubai den hon 40% o mot so noi Tay Au.

Chi phi sinh hoat anh huong den so FIRE cua toi nhu the nao?

So FIRE cua ban bang 25 lan chi phi hang nam. Chuyen tu thanh pho $5.000/thang sang thanh pho $2.500/thang cat so FIRE tu $1,5 trieu xuong $750.000 — giam khoang mot nua thoi gian den doc lap tai chinh.

Chenh lech dia ly la gi?

Chenh lech dia ly co nghia la kiem bang dong tien manh trong khi song o noi co chi phi thap hon. Nguoi lam viec tu xa su dung dieu nay de tiet kiem nhieu hon, dau tu nhieu hon va dat doc lap tai chinh nhanh hon. Tim hieu them ve chenh lech dia ly.
