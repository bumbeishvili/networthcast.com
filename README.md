# NetWorthCast — Net Worth Tracker & Forecast

**Your wealth. Your timeline. Your move.**

NetWorthCast is a free, all-in-one financial planning platform that combines net worth tracking, financial forecasting, FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) planning, and cost-of-living comparison tools — all running entirely in your browser with zero server communication.

## What Is NetWorthCast?

Whether you're tracking your net worth, forecasting growth over the next 30 years, calculating your FIRE number, or comparing costs of living across 44 cities worldwide — NetWorthCast does it all in one place, for free, with no account required.

## Core Tools & Features

### 1. Net Worth Tracker
Track all your assets in one place — liquid (cash, savings, stocks, crypto, bonds) and illiquid (real estate, vehicles, collectibles). Set individual expected return rates per asset. Monitor your total net worth in real time with drag-and-drop reordering and multi-currency support.

**URL:** [/net-worth-tracker/](https://networthcast.com/net-worth-tracker/)

### 2. Net Worth Calculator
Calculate your total net worth by summing assets and subtracting liabilities. Get a clear snapshot of where you stand financially, broken down by asset type and category.

**URL:** [/net-worth-calculator/](https://networthcast.com/net-worth-calculator/)

### 3. Net Worth Forecast
Project your net worth up to 30 years into the future. Uses compound growth calculations per asset, supports recurring income/expenses with annual growth rates, one-time financial events, and asset-linked income streams. Explore your timeline with an interactive slider and visual chart.

**URL:** [/net-worth-forecast/](https://networthcast.com/net-worth-forecast/)

### 4. FIRE Calculator
Calculate your FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) number based on the 4% safe withdrawal rate (annual expenses × 25). Supports multiple FIRE strategies:
- **Lean FIRE** — Minimalist retirement (~$40k/year expenses)
- **Coast FIRE** — When your investments alone will grow to your FIRE target
- **Barista FIRE** — Partial retirement with part-time income
- **Fat FIRE** — Comfortable retirement ($100k+/year expenses)

**URL:** [/fire-calculator/](https://networthcast.com/fire-calculator/)

### 5. Coast FIRE Calculator
Determine when you can stop actively saving and let your existing investments compound to your FIRE target by retirement age. Calculates the exact coast number and how far along you are.

**URL:** [/coast-fire-calculator/](https://networthcast.com/coast-fire-calculator/)

### 6. Cost of Living Comparison
Compare living costs across **44 cities in 11 world regions**. Covers rent, food, transportation, utilities, healthcare, and miscellaneous expenses. Factors in local tax rates on remote income and scales costs by family size (single, couple, family).

**Cities include:** New York, Austin, Miami, Toronto, Vancouver, London, Paris, Berlin, Amsterdam, Zurich, Dublin, Milan, Barcelona, Lisbon, Split, Warsaw, Budapest, Prague, Tallinn, Sofia, Bucharest, Belgrade, Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Bali, Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, Kuala Lumpur, Tokyo, Seoul, Taipei, Dubai, Istanbul, Bangalore, Goa, Tbilisi, Yerevan, Almaty, Mexico City, Medellin, Buenos Aires, Sao Paulo, Playa del Carmen, Sydney, Auckland, Cape Town

**URL:** [/cost-of-living-comparison/](https://networthcast.com/cost-of-living-comparison/)

### 7. Geographic Arbitrage Calculator
Calculate how relocating to a lower-cost city can accelerate your path to financial independence. Compare current expenses vs. destination costs, factor in local tax rates for remote workers, and estimate years saved to reach FIRE.

**URL:** [/geographic-arbitrage/](https://networthcast.com/geographic-arbitrage/)

### 8. Where to Live Calculator
Find the best city for your financial goals. Filter and rank 44 cities based on your income, expenses, lifestyle preferences, and FIRE timeline. Get personalized recommendations based on affordability and quality of life metrics.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**What is net worth?**
Net worth is the total value of everything you own (assets) minus everything you owe (liabilities). It includes liquid assets like cash, savings, stocks, and crypto, plus illiquid assets like real estate and vehicles.

**How do I calculate my net worth?**
Add up all your assets (bank accounts, investments, property, vehicles, retirement accounts) and subtract all your debts (mortgage, loans, credit card balances). The result is your net worth.

**What is the FIRE movement?**
FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It's a lifestyle movement focused on aggressive saving and investing to achieve financial independence and retire much earlier than the traditional age of 65.

**How do I calculate my FIRE number?**
Your FIRE number is typically your annual expenses multiplied by 25 (based on the 4% safe withdrawal rate). For example, if you spend $40,000 per year, your FIRE number is $1,000,000.

**What is Coast FIRE?**
Coast FIRE is the point where your existing investments, without any additional contributions, will grow to your full FIRE number by your target retirement age through compound growth alone. Once you reach Coast FIRE, you only need to cover current expenses.

**What is Lean FIRE vs Fat FIRE?**
Lean FIRE means reaching financial independence on a minimalist budget (typically under $40,000/year in expenses). Fat FIRE means achieving FI with a more comfortable lifestyle budget ($100,000+/year). Both use the same 4% rule but with different spending targets.

**What is geographic arbitrage?**
Geographic arbitrage means earning income in a high-cost economy (like the US or Western Europe) while living in a lower-cost location. This dramatically increases your savings rate and can cut years off your FIRE timeline.

**Is my financial data safe on NetWorthCast?**
Yes. All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No data is ever sent to any server. There are no accounts, no cloud storage, and no third-party data sharing. Your financial information never leaves your device.

**Does NetWorthCast work offline?**
Yes. As a Progressive Web App (PWA), NetWorthCast can be installed on your device and works offline for cached pages and functionality.

**Is NetWorthCast really free?**
Yes, completely free. No premium features, no paywalls, no trial periods, no subscriptions. All tools are available to everyone at no cost.
